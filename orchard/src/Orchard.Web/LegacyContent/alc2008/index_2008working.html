GIVE TO RICE

 


You are invited to join an exclusive group of alumni for the 2008 Conference for Rice Alumni Leaders, to be held April 25-26 on the Rice campus. This weekend brings together the best and brightest of our fellow alumni—those whose commitment and passion for Rice and our greater community exemplify the leadership and dedication to service that is changing our world.

Involved as a student or active as an alum, you are invited to lead the way as Rice continues on the unparalleled journey charted by its founders. Hear about cutting-edge research and the challenging academic landscape. Learn how faculty and students are finding creative solutions to society's most pressing challenges. Mingle with deans, vice presidents, and other university leaders as you get an insider's view and share ideas. Visit with students about Rice today and strengthen your connection with fellow alumni leaders.

This is an invitation to aspire and achieve. To discover again Rice's uncommon drive toward innovation. To experience the ambition and vision first set forth by Edgar Odell Lovett, who proposed "no upper limit" to our educational endeavors. Join us and lead.

Register online by Friday, April 18, and see below for the conference schedule and details.

Dear Rice alumni leader,

The history of any great institution is one of great leadership. As we approach Rice's centennial, we celebrate the unique accomplishments of our alma mater while preparing for the challenges ahead. As a leader and volunteer with the university, you play a key role in implementing Rice's Vision for the Second Century.

We invite you to join us at the 2008 Conference for Rice Alumni Leaders. Held every three years, this conference brings together the best and brightest of our alumni and friends to receive an "insider's view" of Rice from campus leadership. This year's conference focuses on the new initiatives at Rice that are changing the way we lead the nation and the world. We also have recruited some of your fellow alumni to share how their Rice experience shaped them as leaders and what challenges they face in their respective fields. We hope this opportunity for personal leadership learning will complement the updates you'll receive from campus communicators. This will be your chance to mix and mingle with vice presidents, deans, faculty, and institute heads, and to hear from President Leebron.

Join us as we celebrate Rice's innovative spirit and rediscover the commitment and connections of everyone involved with leading Rice into a bold future with "no upper limit."

Maryana Iskander '97
Jeffery O. Rose '77
Conference Chairs
