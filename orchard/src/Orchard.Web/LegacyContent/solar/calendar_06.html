December 2006

Friday, December 8, 2006
Holiday Posada and Reception
Hosted by Dorothy Caram '55/'74 at her home
6:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.

Traditional Posada treats will be served including tamales and atole. RSVP to SOLAR by Thursday, December 7, at 5:00 p.m.

November 2006

Friday, November 17, 2006
Homecoming Reception and Annual Meeting
Miner Lounge and Meyer Conference Room,
Rice Memorial Center
6:30 p.m. to 9:30 p.m.

Elections for the board of directors will be held during the reception. The First Annual Outstanding Latino Alumni Award will be presented.

Please email any questions to SOLAR.

October 2006

Thursday, October 5, 2006
Wine Tasting Scholarship Fundraiser:
"Wines of the Americas"
O'Connor House, Rice University (NEW VENUE!)
6:30 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.

Learn about and experience the wines of Argentina, Chile and Texas! Proceeds from the event will fund the "SOLAR Leadership Award" scholarship for undergraduates.

Rice Alumni $30
Rice Students $20
Guests $40

Space is limited! RSVP to SOLAR by September 30.

September 2006

Friday, September 29, 2006
Shell Hispanic Employee Network Learning Conference
McNair Hall, Rice University
8:00 to 11:35 a.m.
No registration fee

SOLAR members are invited to attend the morning workshops and keynote speech on career development. Featured speakers include John Phillip Santos, author and first Mexican-American Rhodes Scholar, and Massey Villarreal, chair of the TxED Governing Board.

Space is limited!!! To receive further information or to register by Sept. 25, email SOLAR.
