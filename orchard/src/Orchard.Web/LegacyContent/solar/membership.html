Dear Prospective Member:

SOLAR is a membership-based organization. Please send in your dues payment and support SOLAR in its endeavors. Your dues help fund the scholarships and provide a quality and diverse program of activities throughout the year. Participating in alumni activities is a selfless way to give back to the community and campus, and you have a chance to reconnect with fellow alumni. Join us and be a part of the excitement and tradition SOLAR is creating.

Dues are payable annually on or about September 1 of every year. Membership from the prior year shall be valid for up to 90 days after September 1. SOLAR does not pro-rate membership dues, but all membership applications received in the 90 days prior to September 1 shall be automatically treated as valid members for the next membership year. All Latino alumni of Rice are encouraged to become members, regardless of dues.

SOLAR is a separate, nonprofit organization. While its membership is comprised of alumni of Rice University, donations to SOLAR are neither counted as a donation to nor managed by the university.
