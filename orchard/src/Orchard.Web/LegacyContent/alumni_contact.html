Rice Career Network (formerly the Alumni Contact Service)

  • Are you thinking about changing careers? Have you moved to a new city? There might be alumni who can help you!
  • Do you enjoy helping people, especially fellow alums and Rice students? We need you!

The Rice Career Network is a searchable database of alumni who have agreed to make themselves available to Rice students and to fellow alums for information about their job field, company, graduate degree institution, city of residence, and the like.

Rice alumni and students can use the RCN online (registration required). The terms of use of the RCN are given here.

To join the Rice Career Network as a volunteer contact, please visit the online RCN and select "Volunteer" from the menu.
