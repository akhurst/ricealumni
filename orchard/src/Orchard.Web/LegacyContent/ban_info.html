Builder's Award

To honor OUTSTANDING young alumni volunteers!

The Builder's Award is the highest honor that can be presented to a young alumni volunteer. It is presented at the annual Builder's Award ceremony each spring. The ceremony includes a chance for mingling and meeting other Rice young alums, fabulous food at an exciting location, as well as a keynote speaker. It is a great time to be had by all, and an event definitely not to be missed!

2012 Builder's Award Reception

Thursday, May 17, 2012
Coronado Club
919 Milam St # 500
7:00 p.m.

This year's Builder's Award will be held in conjunction with the Rice Annual Fund's Cenntennial Challenge to Young Alumni Reception.

Call for Nominations

Please submit nominations for the 2012 Builder's Award, the highest honor that can be presented to a young alumni volunteer serving the Rice community. Take a few minutes to nominate a deserving young alum. Nomination deadline: Friday, April 6.

Nomination eligibility

  • The recipient(s) must have graduated from Rice within the last fifteen (15) years. In 2012, alumni from the classes of 1997-2011 are eligible nominees.
  • The recipient(s) must demonstrate significant, sustained, voluntary contributions of energy, time, and creativity towards the advancement of the University.
  • The recipient's contributions may involve any (or multiple) departments, colleges, associations, events, offices, athletic arenas, or any other areas as the Selection Committee deems worthy.

Selection Committee

The Builder's Award Selection Committee is comprised of the Selection Committee chair, the HYAC chair, alumni office staff liaison, one faculty or staff member, and up to two other HYAC committee members.
