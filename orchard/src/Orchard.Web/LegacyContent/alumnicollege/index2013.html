Rice University Alumni College Weekend

What is Alumni College Weekend?

Since 1995, the Association of Rice Alumni has held this weekend-long event inviting alumni, parents, and friends to campus for an experience that is uniquely Rice. Join us for a series of classes with some of our best and brightest professors and rising stars. The Association of Rice Alumni firmly believes that the pursuit of knowledge should continue beyond your time as a student, and Alumni College Weekend is a fabulous opportunity to expand your intellectual horizons. And when you’re not in class, the program offers a chance to mix, mingle, and network with your fellow "classmates" — a broad spectrum of Rice community members.

Scholarship

The Leo S. Shamblin Scholarship Fund was established to provide scholarship support to potential Alumni College participants with a strong record of volunteer service in the community and on campus. Recipients must plan to participate in the entire Alumni College Weekend, and young alumni and out-of-town participants are especially encouraged to apply. Individuals interested in applying for the scholarship funds should email a letter to Carrie Brown explaining why the funds would assist in attending Alumni College Weekend and how they satisfy the criteria regarding volunteer service.
