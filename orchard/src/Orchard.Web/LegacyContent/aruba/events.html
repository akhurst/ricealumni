“The Blueprint for Excellence: A Centennial Celebration of Achievement"
Association of Rice University Black Alumni
(formerly known as the “Blacks in Blue” event)

Friday, October 12, 2012
6:30pm
La Colombe d’Or - Grand Salon
Houston, Texas

Please plan to join your fellow Rice ARUBA members, Alumni, Administration and Community for the Friday signature event during the 2012 Rice Centennial celebration. The event will recognize the outstanding achievements of Rice Black Alumni and honor those who were instrumental to the initiatives of diversity and Black students at Rice University.

For those who would like to participate/volunteer to assist with the planning of the event, please send an email with your interest and commitment to Angela Berry Roberson at angieberry@alumni.rice.edu or feel free to contact the Committee Chairs listed below. The event planning committees are:

  • Awards and Programming: Akilah Mance '05 and Jan West ’73
  • Budget and Sponsorships: Kenitra Brown '07
  • Logistics- Pretta Vandible:Stallworth ’83, ’85
  • Entertainment: Stephen McVea ’89
  • Marketing and PR: Jasmine Gipson ’02
