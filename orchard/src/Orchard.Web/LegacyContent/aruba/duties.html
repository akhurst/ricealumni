Association of Rice University Black Alumni (ARUBA)

Duties of Officers and Committee Chairs

Chair

The Chair shall serve a two-year term and:

  1. Preside over the general and Executive Committee meetings;
  2. Supervise operations of ARUBA;
  3. Recommend chairpersons for ad-hoc and subcommittees to the Executive Committee;
  4. Delegate duties or assign projects to Committees as deemed appropriate;
  5. Serve as an ex-officio member of all committees;
  6. Attend at least 75% of all meetings;
  7. Present an Annual Report to the ARUBA general body at the annual meeting during Homecoming;
  8. Serve as Immediate-Past President upon completion of term;
  9. Shall not be permitted to serve consecutive terms;
  10. Take an active role in local alumni activities and in promoting ARUBA and Rice involvement to fellow alumni in their geographic region; and
  11. Shall reside in Houston if the Chair-elect resides outside of the Greater Houston area (vice versa, if applicable). The residence of the Chair and the Chair-elect should be such that one resides in Houston and the other resides outside the Greater Houston area.

Chair-Elect

The Chair-Elect shall serve a two-year term and:

  1. Serve in the absence of the President;
  2. Serve as Acting President in the event that the President is unable to fulfill the duties of his/her office as consistent with these bylaws;
  3. Must attend 75% of all meetings;
  4. Serve as an ex-officio member of the Nominations Committee;
  5. Serve as President upon completion of term as President-Elect;
  6. Take an active role in local alumni activities and in promoting ARUBA and Rice involvement to fellow alumni in their geographic region; and
  7. Shall reside in Houston if the Chair resides outside of the greater Houston area (vice versa, if applicable). As stated earlier, the residence of the Chair and the Chair-elect should be such that one resides in Houston and the other resides outside the Greater Houston area.

At -Large Board Members (at least 4)

The At-Large members should consist of at least the following ARUBA members: at least one from the Greater Houston, at least one from Texas but outside the Greater Houston area, and at least one member which resides outside of Texas. The At-Large Board members should serve a two-year term and:

  1. Take an active role in local alumni activities and in promoting ARUBA and Rice involvement to fellow alumni in their geographic region
  2. Spearhead at least two regional ARUBA activities per year: at least one regional ARUBA activity for their local ARUBA members and at least one ARUBA activity for the future and current black students of the University (and their Parents);
  3. Assist the Executive Committee and the committee chairs with the implementation and execution of ARUBA objectives and initiatives in their particular geographic region;
  4. Provide input to the Executive Committee about ARUBA members and activities in their geographic region;
  5. Become an active committee member on at least one of ARUBA standing committees; and
  6. Attend the ARUBA general meeting during Homecoming.

 Standing Committees Chairs

Committee Chairs shall be appointed for a term of two years. Chairpersons may serve consecutive terms upon recommendation of the Chair and approval of the Executive Committee.

  1. Standing committee chairs assist the Executive Committee with execution of the mission and objectives of the organization.
  2. Must be present at a majority of all Executive Committee meetings and
  3. Shall prepare status reports for both the Executive committee and the General Body at least on an annual basis.
  4. Take an active role in local alumni activities and in promoting ARUBA and Rice involvement to fellow alumni in their geographic region
