Association of Rice University Black Alumni (ARUBA)

Home | Calendar | News | Interact!

ARUBA seeks to provide a forum for black graduates of Rice University to develop and/or maintain relationships with each other and with the Rice University community as a means of adding value to the educational experience.

Our objectives are:

Assisting current students with assimilation into campus life and facilitating opportunities to enhance the academic experience;

Participating in recruiting activities of both minority students and faculty to promote a more diverse campus culture;
Providing networking and career development opportunities for minority alumni by capitalizing on resources within the organization;
Cultivating and enhancing relationships within the organization through socializing.

To stay up-to-date electronically on ARUBA news and events, please bookmark this page and join our email broadcast list.

For discussion, join our Yahoo! group, arubaonline.

If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to contact Association of Rice Alumni board member Brandy Hays '00, '05.
