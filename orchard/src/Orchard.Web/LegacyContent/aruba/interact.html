Mailing list

Rice sends emails and invitations to ARUBA members and friends for whom we have contact information. If you aren't receiving information about ARUBA activities and would like to, please check your information in the online alumni directory and make sure it is correct. If so but you're not hearing from us, please contact the alumni office to let us know you'd like to receive information about ARUBA.

For reminders and last-minute updates on ARUBA news and events, please join our email broadcast list. This does not replace official emails from ARUBA or the alumni office, but is for informal communications from the group.

Online forum

Join the members of ARUBA online for informal discussion of club activities, local events, and any other topics of interest to the grou. We're on Yahoo! at arubaonline.
