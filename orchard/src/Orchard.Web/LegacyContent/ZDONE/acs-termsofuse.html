Rice Career Network: Terms and Conditions

Before using the Rice Career Network (formerly the Alumni Contact Service), please read and be aware of the following:

The Rice Career Network (RCN) is available for authorized use by Rice University students, alumni, faculty and staff. Your failure to use the RCN for its intended purposes will preclude your further use of the service.

Authorized use of the RCN is limited to the following:

You may use the listings to request information from alumni in career areas in which you are interested.
You may use the listings to identify alumni in geographic locations to which you may be moving. These alumni contacts may be used to gather information on a region's labor market, hiring trends, or major employers. You may also contact alumni for tips on relocation to their area.
You may identify alumni working in companies in which you are interested in order to learn about company culture, hiring trends or major employers.
You may contact alumni with particular majors to brainstorm possible career alternatives.
You may use the Rice Career Network to identify alumni who may offer leads for securing employment.

Unauthorized use of the RCN includes the following:

You may not contact an alum to directly request a job.
You may not use the RCN to develop a list of alumni for solicitation (i.e., sales or fundraising leads).
Any other purposes not expressly stated as authorized use or otherwise approved by the Career Services Center or Alumni Office.

Clearly state to any alum that you found his or her name in the Rice Career Network and be sure the alum understands your relation to Rice (i.e. alum/student/staff).

Remember — RCN contacts are very often not in a position to hire. They are working people who have expressed a willingness to talk about their jobs to other Rice people. Please keep this in mind when you get in touch with them.
