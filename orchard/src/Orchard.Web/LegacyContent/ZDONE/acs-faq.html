RICE CAREER NETWORK (formerly the Alumni Contact Service)

GENERAL QUESTIONS

  		• How do I get started as a user?

    First, you'll need to sign up for an online services account if you haven't already. Next, visit the main Rice Career Network page. On your first visit, you must read and agree to the Terms of Use to continue.

    Begin your search, remembering that the results will match all fields. If you don't find what you're looking for, try widening your search. When you see a promising contact, add that person to your Notebook. This will save the name for later review while you continue searching. When you're ready to make contact with a person, view your Notebook and choose "Activate Now," which will give you access to the person's contact information. You may store as many names in the Notebook as you want, but you are allowed to contact only a limited number of alumni per month.

    		• I'm looking for a job. Can I send these contacts my resume?
    The Rice Career Network is not a job placement service, and you are prohibited by the Terms of Use from soliciting employment from our volunteers. But if you are conducting a job search, our volunteers can help you research a particular industry, company, or geographic region, and they will have valuable advice on numerous employment topics.
    		• Why are search results randomized?
    We want to ensure that the same few names aren't always at the top of the search results and thus contacted more frequently than others. If you are looking for a particular name within a search result, you can click "Name" at the top of the column and the results will be sorted alphabetically. The other columns can also be sorted in the same manner.
    		• Why is there a limit to the number of contacts I can retrieve?
    Our volunteers have agreed to be contacted only so often. We limit you to a certain number of contacts per month to give other users equal access. Although our volunteers' contact information may be available from other sources, we ask that you respect this limit and our volunteers' privacy.
    		• What should I say when I call or write alumni?
    Relax! These alumni have volunteered for this, and will be happy to hear from you. Identify yourself as a Rice student or alumnus, and mention that you received the name from the Rice Career Network. If you are writing a letter, be precise about what advice you are seeking. Before making a phone call, prepare a list of your most important questions. At the end of your conversation, ask if you can call again should you have additional questions. Always present yourself in a professional manner, even in email correspondence. And be sure to thank the volunteers for their time!

    VOLUNTEERING
  		• How do I sign up as a volunteer?

    First of all, thank you! You'll need to sign up for an online services account if you haven't already. Next, visit the main Rice Career Network page. On your first visit, you must read and agree to the Terms of Use to continue.

    Follow the link to "Volunteer." Read the introduction and agree to continue. You'll be asked to fill out some employment information. At this time, you can also decide the frequency with which you want to be contacted. Once this limit has been reached, your name will not appear in future RCN searches for a period of time. Please also verify the accuracy of your online directory information, which will be displayed at the bottom of the page.

    Hit the button to "Update Profile" and review the information and settings. Once the changes are accepted, you will be listed in the RCN database.
    		• I'm going on vacation, and I'd like to make my information temporarily unavailable.
    Easily done! From the main RCN page, choose "Update My Profile" and change your status from "active" to "inactive." Changing your status does not affect the rest of your profile or settings. Simply switch the status back when you return.
    		• I've (moved/changed jobs) and I need to update my information.
    From the main RCN page, choose "Update My Profile" and alter your information as needed. For address changes, follow the link toward the bottom of the page to edit your online directory information.
