ARA Strategic Plan

Supporting Students

Provide programs that engage alumni in the university.s essential mission: educating students from pre-admission through commencement.
Establish and manage programs for and with students to develop their affinity to the ARA.
Assist students in recognizing the beginning of a lifelong relationship with Rice.

I. Enhance awareness of the ARA and the Rice legacy for prospective, new, and continuing students through
programs and events appropriately designed for this audience. Expand the network of graduating students
interested in joining the ARA by enhancing their affinity to the association and the university.
  A. Research the undergraduate and graduate admissions process to determine if ARA programs can
enhance connections between pre-admitted students and alumni.
 
  1. Work with undergraduate admissions in continuing to strengthen the alumni and student experience
with Rice Alumni Volunteer Association (RAVA).
  B. Represent the alumni message in programming from orientation through graduation.
  C. Expand and strengthen the Student-Alumni Liaison Committee to encompass building student affinity to
the ARA and identifying the ARA.s next group of volunteers.
 
  1. Expand alumni mentoring programs for undergraduate and graduate students.
2. Explore and implement new technology that can assist in providing programming to increase student
affinity to the ARA.
3. Work with the Student Association, student groups, and the residential colleges to create student
affinity to the ARA before students leave Rice.
4. Work with the Graduate Student Association and graduate apartments to determine how ARA
programming might enhance the graduate student experience and to develop a graduate-student affinity
to the ARA before students leave Rice.
  D. Assist the residential colleges and graduate apartments in improving the alumni associate experience, for both the students and alumni involved.
