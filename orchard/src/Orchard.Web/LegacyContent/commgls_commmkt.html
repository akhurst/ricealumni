Committee Goals 2003–2004

Communications and Marketing

1) Complete guidelines for the use of the ARA logo by December 31, 2003

2) Complete space plan for O'Connor House by June 30, 2004.

3) Meet with Public Affairs (Terry Shepard) to assess the potential to increase the alumni circulation of the @Rice electronic newsletter. To be done by November 30, 2003.

4) Develop a plan for improvement of ARA "advertising" in the Owlmanac and Rice News by December 31, 2003.

5) Hold two focus groups on the functionality of the electronic alumni database. The first group should meet before November 30, 2003 and the second before April 30, 2004.

6) Hold one focus group on the functionality of the ARA website by December 31, 2003.

7) Develop a set of metrics to measure and track the success of ARA programs by February 28, 2004.
