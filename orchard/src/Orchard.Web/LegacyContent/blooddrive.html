Campus-wide "Gift of Life" Blood Drive
March 27–29, 2006

The Association of Rice Alumni, the residential colleges and the Rice Student Volunteer Program, in conjunction with the Gulf Coast Regional Blood Center, will sponsor blood drives on the Rice University campus March 27–29. The ARA offers cash prizes and plaques to the colleges generating the most donated units of blood in their drives.

In the fall of 2005 a similar drive generated 233 units of whole blood. Sid Richardson College won the $125 first place prize in the ARA sponsored competition, with Brown College receiving a second place cash award of $75.

The drive is open generally to everyone over 17 years of age in good health. Rice alumni, faculty, and staff have always played an important part in these drives. Bring a photo ID with you to the donation site of your choice.

Donations may also be made at any office of the Gulf Coast Regional Blood Center. For the location nearest you, call 713-790-1200 or visit www.giveblood.org. Mention that you are part of the Association of Rice Alumni blood drive or name the college you wish to support. The center can also answer donor eligibility questions if you have lived outside the U.S. or are taking certain medications.

For more information, please contact Alumni Blood Drive Coordinator Jim Greenwood '58 at 713-659-8466 or Andrea Johnson with the Gulf Coast Regional Blood Center at 713-791-6236.
