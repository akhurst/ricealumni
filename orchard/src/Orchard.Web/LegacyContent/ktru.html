KTRU Links

In mid-August Rice University and the University of Houston announced that their respective boards had reached an agreement for the University of Houston System to purchase Rice's 50,000-watt radio frequency and broadcast tower for use by Houston's local public broadcasting station, KUHF. Rice's station, KTRU, will continue to operate a Web-based radio station at www.ktru.org.  Please see below for links to emails to alumni, FAQ, and articles.
