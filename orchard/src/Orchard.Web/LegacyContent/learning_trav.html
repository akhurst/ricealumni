The mission of the Association of Rice Alumni includes a commitment to lifelong learning. As part of this effort, Rice Alumni is pleased to offer the following services and programs:

Alumni College — An annual spring weekend on the Rice campus with non-credit classes developed especially for alumni and friends. Alumni College Weekend 2012 will take place March 9-11. Program details will be posted soon!
Rice Alumni Travel Program — Trips of great value to trips of a lifetime: travel the world in the good company of fellow Owls, families, and friends.
Glasscock School of Continuing Studies — Alumni are eligible for discounts on many courses.

Rice Alumni Digital Library — Full-text access to more than 14,500 academic, trade, and professional publications from ebrary® as well as business, news, and science and engineering publications from ProQuest®. (Online Services registration required.)

Scholarships and Fellowships — Alumni are eligible for select scholarships coordinated through Rice.
Connexions — A project developed and housed at Rice, Connexions allows authors, educators, and learners to access a rich repository of open-licensed content modules to create free, customized textbooks, courses, and learning materials. Perfect for either the casual at-home learner or a teacher looking for educational resources.
