Nomination criteria
Those nominated should be Rice alumni who have demonstrate exceptional volunteer service to Rice over the past 12 months through time and effort towards the enhancement of the university. They may not have received an ARA award in the past three years.

To be considered for the upcoming group of Volunteer Spotlight alumni, nominations must be received by Friday, October 29; however, nominations are always accepted.
