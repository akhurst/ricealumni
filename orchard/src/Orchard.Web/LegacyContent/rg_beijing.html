Sign up for the Beijing Alumni Listserv

We have created a local email listserv for Rice alumni and friends. This does not take the place of official communications that are sent to all local alumni and parents through the alumni office. For those who wish to sign up, the listserv will provide an opportunity for more informal communication among Rice community members, who can post activities or opportunities that may be of interest to fellow Owls.

For more information and to opt in to this moderated list, please visit https://mailman.rice.edu/mailman/listinfo/beijing-owls-l.
