Stay in touch electronically with @Rice
@Rice is a monthly newsletter about campus prepared by Rice University's Division of Public Affairs, Office of Media Relations and Information.

Alumni and parents of current Rice students automatically receive @Rice if they have provided their email to Rice and if they have not unsubscribed from receiving email communication from the university. Alumni can update their email and contact information through the online alumni directory.

To SUBSCRIBE or UNSUBSCRIBE, send an email to the administrator. In the body of the email, type your request (subscribe or unsubscribe), your first name, middle initial, last name and class year.
