Class Selection

Alumni College Weekend is $295 per person, and a Saturday-only pass is $165 per person—or $95 for young alumni who graduated since 2000. The cost includes course fees, meals, and parking. Early-bird registration concludes on February 10. Upon receipt of your registration we will send you confirmation and details to facilitate your arrival and check-in. Registration received after the February 10 deadline will be $325 for the weekend program, $175 for the Saturday-only pass, and $105 for the young alumni Saturday-only pass. All classes are filled on a first-come, first-served basis.
