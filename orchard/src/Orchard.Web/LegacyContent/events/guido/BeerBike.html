Don't make your team lose by not playing! Welcome to the RICE Alumni Online Beer Bike Race! This is our first effort to take this unique event virtually to allow alumni from all over the country to participate in the upcoming festivities. You can help your college win a post-race party sponsored by the Association of Rice Alumni by simply voting for your favorite team below!

All you have to do is enter your email address and college affiliation below to register one hit for that college. The more hits a college gets, the better its standings will be relative to the other colleges. The winning college will be announced on the alumni website on March 20, 2003.
