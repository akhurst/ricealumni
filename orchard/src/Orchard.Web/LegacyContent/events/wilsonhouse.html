This reception honors Tom & Kate Wilson, who were instrumental in helping to preserve Bill's legacy at Rice.  But the idea for the reception originates with Dr. Bill himself.  In the 1980s and 1990s, Dr. Bill would host informal Thursday happy hours during the summer at his RA suite, at which alumni (as well as students who were in town) were welcome to stop by.  These events were a fun mix of people of many different class years, united by their connection to Rice and their friendship with Dr. Bill.  The summer happy hours were just one of the many ways in which Dr. Bill served as a living link who connected students and alumni to Rice, to Wiess and to each other.  We hope that this reception in some small way evokes fond memories of those gatherings, and of the warm and generous man who hosted them.
