Houston Young Alumni Presidential Reception

More than 200 young alumni from the Houston area attended an evening reception featuring President David Leebron on October 25, 2004. The event was held at the Museum of Fine Arts, Houston, in the foyer of the Audrey Jones Beck Building and was hosted by the Association of Rice Alumni, the Houston Young Alumni Committee, and a host committee of more than 35 alumni.
