Upcoming Dallas and Fort Worth Events

Click to view events in Dallas or Fort Worth, or visit the event calendar for other upcoming alumni events.

Please note: If you live in the DFW area and are not receiving our monthly email updates, it means we do not have your current email address in our system. Please take a moment to update your email by visiting the online alumni directory.

Sign up for the DFW Alumni Listserv

We have created a new local email listserv for Rice alumni. This does not take the place of monthly emails that are sent to all local alumni and friends through the alumni office. For those who wish to sign up, the listserv will provide an opportunity for more informal communication among alumni, who can post activities or opportunities that may be of interest to fellow alums.

For more information and to opt in to this new moderated list, please visit https://mailman.rice.edu/mailman/listinfo/dfw-owls-l.
