Princeton Club of New York
Affiliate Membership —
An Opportunity for Rice Alumni

Do you travel to, live or work in New York City? If so, the Princeton Club of New York may be perfect for you. The nine-story clubhouse provides the opportunity to experience the best of New York in comfortable and pleasant surroundings — all while staying connected with fellow alumni.

Conveniently located in the heart of midtown Manhattan, minutes from Grand Central Terminal, Times Square, Rockefeller Center, the Theater District … and Fifth Avenue shopping!

Whether your needs are, business or social, the wide array of exclusive educational, cultural, and networking programs will allow you to interact with a diverse group of individuals that share similar interests. Membership is very reasonable and practically pays for itself; be it with just a few overnight stays in Manhattan or at any of the 110 private reciprocal clubs worldwide.

Club amenities include:

  • 50 affordable and renovated hotel rooms
  • Formal and casual dining and bar
  • Banquet and meeting facilities
  • Business center and free WiFi
  • Lounges and library
  • Squash and fitness facility
  • Full program of events
  • Special arrangements with transportation services, private golf courses, top retailers, and local parking facilities

Membership is open to Rice University alumni, faculty, and staff. To learn more about membership, schedule a personal tour, or to receive an information packet, please visit the Princeton Club of New York website or contact the membership office at 212-596-1240.

For more information on joining the Princeton Club and to download an application, visit http://www.princetonclub.com/ricealumni.
