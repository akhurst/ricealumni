2005 Travel/Study Photo Contest

Congratulations to Mary Kay Manning Smith ’62, whose Mykonos Moment was awarded "Best of Show" in the 2005 Rice Alumni Travel/Photo Contest. Smith will receive two certificates totaling $500 to apply toward future travel with the Rice Alumni Travel/Study Program. Judges awarded "Honorable Mention" to a second photo by Smith, and to entries from Rice parent and traveler Susan Myrick, and alumni Joe Hess ’68, Philip Seeger ’58, and Frank Shelden ’48. Congratulations to all winners, and our thanks to all who entered our inaugural photo contest.

Photos will be on display at a reception and silent auction honoring all finalists in the 6th Annual International Programs Photo Contest (including both student and alumni entries), to be held on campus on Monday, March 21. Photos will remain on display in Farnsworth Pavilion (in the Ley Student Center) through April 15. Proceeds from the auction benefit Rice International Programs. Reprints of winning photos--in both the alumni and student categories--may also be purchased online through the International Programs website.

If you will be traveling with the Rice Alumni Travel/Study Program this year, don’t forget that future photo contests run on a calendar-year basis. Entries for our 2006 contest—for which only photos from 2005 trips are eligible—will be due at the end of January 2006. Complete rules and an entry form are available here.
