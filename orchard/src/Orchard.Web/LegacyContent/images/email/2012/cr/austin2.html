The First 100 Years

Distinguished storytellers from Austin and John Boles '65, the William P. Hobby Professor of History, will regale the crowd with stories of Rice’s past and present.

The Next 100 Years

President David Leebron will illustrate how Rice continues to pursue founding president Edgar Odell Lovett’s vision for "no upper limit."

Enter the Distinguished Storyteller Competition for the chance to be flown to Houston for Rice's Centennial Celebration Oct. 10-14! The Centennial Story Booth will be on location at the event to record your Rice story. All entries will be featured on the Centennial Story Project website.
