Houston: Rise to the Challenge is an open invitation for Houston young alumni to make gifts to their alma mater and discover how their philanthropy has an incredible impact on Rice. The Centennial Challenge to Young Alumni seeks to encourage and celebrate young alumni support of the Rice Annual Fund. Co-sponsors Keith Anderson ’83 and Charley Landgraf ’75 are asking young alumni to rise to the challenge in support of Rice and are matching gifts to the Annual Fund until June 30, 2011.

The Annual Fund, a top priority of Rice’s Centennial Campaign, touches every corner of the university and is vital to ensuring that Rice continues to provide academic excellence as well as a high-caliber experience for today’s Owls.
