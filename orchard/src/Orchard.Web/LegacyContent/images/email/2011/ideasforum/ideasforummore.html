The Ideas Forum provides a small-group setting to generate high-level feedback about the university’s greatest opportunities and challenges in assuming global leadership in the 21st century. We request your input and expertise at the following sessions:
Rice can compete successfully in the global race for talented faculty and students by creating collaborative networks that capitalize on our strengths and extend our global reach. This session highlights our international collaborations in quantitative biology, nanotechnology, urban studies and urban health.
This session presents two initiatives — Beyond Traditional Borders and the Rice–Baylor Biomarker Highway — that use technological and entrepreneurial innovation to solve the most urgent moral and economic challenges we face: driving down costs and significantly increasing access to health care.
Addressing the world’s energy needs requires multiple “shots on goal,” and Rice — a global player in nanoscience and materials science that is located in the world’s energy capital — is positioned to lead by creating materials and technologies that will have an immediate impact on the energy market.
