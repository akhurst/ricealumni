Before Rice was Rice, when the campus was but a gleaming idea taking root in an expansive plot of farmland, our founders saw something perfect and got to work constructing a university. Fast forward nearly 100 years, and Rice University is still at work transforming lives and pushing the boundaries of knowledge. To celebrate, we’re gearing up for an once-in-a-lifetime event October 10–14, 2012, that will commemorate the Formal Opening of the Rice Institute in grand style.

Save the date — but keep your calendar open. Over the next several months, you’ll have several opportunities to remember and celebrate the moments that have defined our first 100 years and set the stage for the next 100.
