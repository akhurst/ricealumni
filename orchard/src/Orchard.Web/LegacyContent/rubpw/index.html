Rice University Business and Professional Women (RUBPW)

Rice University Business and Professional Women is a group of mostly Rice women graduates who meet about once a month for dinner in a great Houston restaurant and enjoy networking, visiting, and hearing an interesting speaker on a topic related to business, the Rice community, or to intelligent people in general.

We want to emphasize that you need not be an active career woman to participate. We have members who are not currently working outside the home or who may never have done so. What is important is that you enjoy Rice people and can attend evening meetings.

We meet monthly from September through May with a festive brunch during the holiday season and occasional ventures to museums or theater presentations. You need not be a member to come to a meeting.

September 2009 Program

We begin our year with a special networking session to get to know one another. You'll learn who else is out there and what they are interested in. If you've never been to an RUBPW meeting, we invite you to come fi nd out more about us. Or if you have been before, join us again and renew friendships. Either way, we'd love to see you there!
