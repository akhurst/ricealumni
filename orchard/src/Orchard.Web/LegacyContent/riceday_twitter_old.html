GIVE TO RICE

 

Enter our Rice Day Twitter contest by October 5, 2009! Follow these three easy steps:

  1. Submit your own 140 character (or fewer) description of Rice or the Rice experience using the field below. 140 characters is the maximum number allowed to be “tweeted” on Twitter. Be inspirational and creative! Talk about your college, sports, your classes, your friends, whatever! Limit three entries per alum. Grand prize is your choice of an iPod Nano, iPod Touch, or iPhone!
  2. Sign up to follow the Association of Rice Alumni on Twitter at http://twitter.com/ricealumni. The winning entry will be tweeted on Rice Day, and honorable mentions will be posted to this page.
  3. Be sure to wear your Rice gear on Rice Day, October 12, to let everyone know you’re proud to be an Owl! Click here to learn more about Rice Day.

Conditions
I agree to the contest rules and to allow Rice University and the Association of Rice Alumni to use my name, class year, and contest submission in any and all university print and electronic publications, communications, and websites, including Twitter and Facebook.

Rice Day 2009 Twitter Contest rules:

What is Twitter?
Twitter is a free social networking and micro-blogging service that enables its users to send and read messages known as tweets. Read more about Twitter on Wikipedia.

What is the contest period?
Contest begins September 1, 2009, and ends October 5, 2009.

Who can enter?
Only alumni of Rice University -- those who have attended Rice for at least two semesters in a degree-granting program and are no longer students -- may enter the contest. Current Association of Rice Alumni board members, alumni trustees, alumni office staff, and their immediate families are not eligble. Limit three entries per alum.

How do I enter?
Submit your information using the form above. This is the only way that entries may be submitted. Entries received through the mail, over the phone, or in person will not be permitted.

Who chooses the winner?
The winner will be chosen at the discretion of the Office of Alumni Affairs & University Events in consultation with the Association of Rice Alumni Board of Directors.

What are the prizes?
The grand prize winner will have his/her choice of an iPod Nano (16GB), iPod Touch (8GB), iPhone 3G (8G), or 3GS (16GB). Prize will be in the form of a gift certificate to Apple. Other prizes may be given for honorable mentions and super-fun creativity including Rice T-shirts, hats, tote bags, etc.

The usual disclaimer:
Contestants agree to permit Rice University and the Association of Rice Alumni to use their name, class year, and contest submission in any print publications and online or electronic communications, including Rice University websites, emails, Twitter, Facebook, etc.  Contest rules and prizes subject to change without notice.
