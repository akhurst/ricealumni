2008 Rice Alumni Travel/Study Program

Dear Alumni and Friends:

Our 2008 Rice Alumni Travel/Study Program is one of the largest we have ever offered, with a total of 15 trips to five continents. It includes three different opportunities to visit Italy: a small-ship cruise that circumnavigates the island of Sicily, a week in Tuscany and a cruise that includes a number of Italian ports. In addition to favorite European destinations, Rice travelers will journey to exotic locales such as India, Tahiti and China in the coming year.

Among our offerings this year are several trips that were very successful in previous years and that we are bringing back by popular demand. These include Campus Abroad programs to Ireland and Spain, a cruise along Russian waterways from St. Petersburg to Moscow and a Lewis and Clark adventure trip to Montana and Idaho led by Professor John Boles.

After reading about the trips we have planned for next year, I urge you to complete the online interest form, so you are assured of receiving more detailed information on those that interest you as soon as it is available.

Once again, we will sponsor a photo contest for participants in our travel program. Its purpose is to showcase great photos from Rice trips and to support Rice student travel- and study-abroad programs. You will find more details on this annual contest following the trip descriptions in the back of this brochure.

Also included is information on another opportunity for lifelong learning through the Association of Rice Alumni: the annual Alumni College Weekend program in Houston and Alumni College "On the Road" sessions held in different cities around the country each year.

We hope you will join us for one of these educational opportunities and for the experience that comes with Rice learning and travel.


Sincerely,

Rose Sundin
Associate Director
Association of Rice Alumni
Rice University
