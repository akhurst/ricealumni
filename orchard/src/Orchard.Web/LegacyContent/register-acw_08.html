Class Selection

Alumni College Weekend is $275 per person for the entire weekend, or $150 for the Saturday-only option. The cost includes course fees, meals, and parking. If you would like to bring a guest (non-registrants) to dinner Friday or Saturday night, you may do so for an additional fee of $30 each night. Please be aware that enrollment in Alumni College may be limited by space constraints and will be allocated on a first-come, first-served basis.
