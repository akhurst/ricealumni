Society of Rice University Women (SRUW)

Established in the fall of 1968, the Society of Rice University Women promotes an effective and continuing dialogue between the university and its alumnae. Designed as a women's organization with which all could identify after graduation, SRUW also welcomes female friends of Rice to join and participate.

General meetings and seminars feature Rice professors and staff sharing their latest research and technology, as well as news of what is happening on campus.

Proceeds from our seminars and dues benefit our SRUW Scholarship Endowment Funds for female Rice students, the Fondren Library Woodson Research Center, and the Shepherd School of Music. We are also a source of volunteers for campus activities.

Most meetings and seminars convene in the Parish Hall of Christ the King Lutheran Church, 2353 Rice Blvd. at Greenbriar. Parking is available in the Rice Stadium parking lot.

Membership forms are available at general meetings, or you may download it here as a pdf file. Your membership and seminar participation are very important to our goals, so please join us and renew friendships and memories of your life "beyond the hedges!"
