Letter from the President

Dear San Francisco Bay Area Rice Alumni:

THANK YOU!  Your work on the 2001 Association of Rice Alumni Gift of Life
Blood Drive was terrific.  Thanks, especially to Simon Chen, Janelle Freeman,
Kevin Gass, Virginia Hess, Ming-ye Han and Whit Hughes.  Your work went above
and beyond the call of duty.

Your investment of time, effort and energy, and your donations of blood,
helped save and prolong many lives.  How many we will never know.  At last
count it was clear that you and several hundred Rice Alumni and friends of
Rice from all over the country donated close to 300 units of whole blood and
the tabulating is still incomplete.  THANK YOU.

The Bay Area Rice Alumni group is way ahead of the field in terms of its
efforts and ability to communicate with one another.  Your web site is
fantastic!  Your effort on the blood drive was the best organized and most
sophisticated that I know about outside of Houston.  Keep up the good work.  

One last time, on behalf of the Association of Rice Alumni and the hundreds
of injured and sick fellow humans who are alive and well because of this
drive, Thank you San Francisco Bay Area Rice Alumni!  From the bottoms of our
hearts.

Sincerely,

James (Jim) Greenwood III
President
Association of Rice Alumni
