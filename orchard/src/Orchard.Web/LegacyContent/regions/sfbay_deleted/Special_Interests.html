special interests 


Due to the increasing congestion in the Bay Are, we have made a conscious effort to promote more local events which are narrower in focus. To help foster these groups of interests regionally, we have divide the duties in the SF Bay Area into five distinct areas: San Francisco, Peninsula, Santa Clara/San Jose, Berkeley/East Bay and North Bay. We have assigned an leader in each of the regions to act as a coordinator. Note we are actively looking for a coordinator for the North Bay region. A effort will be made to rotate events throughout the SF Bay Area and each local area coordinator will be the primary point of contact for the events in your local area. Feel free to contact any of the area coordinators if you have any questions or if you would like to help coordinate or participate in an event. We will be using our sf-rice-alumni Yahoo!Groups web site to coordinate the activities.

We are actively look for volunteers to coordinate the groups in each of the regions of the Bay Area. The groups, in no particular order, that we are aiming to develop include:

  • Biking
  • Book Club
  • Community Service
  • Happy Hours
  • Hiking
  • Investment
  • Kayaking
  • Movie
  • Student Send-Off
