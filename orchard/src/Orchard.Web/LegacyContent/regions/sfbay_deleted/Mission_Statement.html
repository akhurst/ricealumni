mission statement


To foster a San Francisco Bay Area Rice Alumni Group (BARAG) community that promotes fellowship and retains strong bonds and relationships back to Rice University.

 

objective


The web site for the Bay Area Rice Alumni Group is intended to act not only as a conduit to inform alumnus of upcoming events but also, more importantly, it will act as a central meeting place to keep in touch with fellow alumnus in the SF Bay Area. In addition to planning events, the local Alumni Group must build a stronger relationship with the University through services we can offer (i.e. mentoring program that benefit both students and/or alumni) the University as well as each Alumni. Finally, since this is the only Alumni Group in Northern California, we would also like reach out and publicize events that are out of the Bay Area.
