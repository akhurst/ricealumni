Mark Your Calendars for July 29, 2001 - Rice Alumni Family Picnic!!!

The Rice Alliance for Technology and Entrepreneurship:

A New Rice University Center

 

Date & Time     Wednesday, April 4, 2001 @ 6:00 PM

 

Reception        Sign-up for the National Rice Alumni Blood Drive

 

Speaker           Steven C. Currall, PhD. (alliance@rice.edu),

                        Director of Rice Alliance for Technology and Entrepreneurship,

                        Associate Professor of Management and Psychology

 

Feedback         Audience Participation and Q&A

 

Location          10435 N. Tantau Avenue (Compaq Cupertino CA HQ Building

                        CAC-07), Cupertino, CA (Note: Entrance and Parking at the

                        Rear of the Bldg. - See Map), tel.408.285.4500

 

R.S.V.P.           Contact Ann Peterson @ tel.800.742.3258 (RICE-ALU)

                        

 

A

ssociation of Rice Alumni (ARA) and the Bay Area Rice Alumni Group (BARAG) invites you to join us in welcoming an interactive presentation on the Rice Alliance for Technology and Entrepreneurship. Find out more about Rice University's strategic effort to build synergies between the Brown School of Engineering, Jones Graduate School of Management and Wiess School of Natural Sciences.  Learn about Rice Alliance's mission to cultivate and assist start-up businesses that germinate from innovations in science, engineering, software and e-commerce by members of the Rice University Community. Participate at this event and see how it can affect you! For more information on how to get involved as a member, mentor or sponsor, please visit the website at www.alliance.rice.edu, contact 713.348.3443 or email alliance@rice.edu.
