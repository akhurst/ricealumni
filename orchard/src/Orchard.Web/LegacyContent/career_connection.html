Networking Opportunities

Rice Career Network
The Rice Career Network (formerly the Alumni Contact Service) is a searchable database of alumni who have agreed to make themselves available to Rice students and to fellow alums for information about their job field, company, graduate degree institution, city of residence, and the like. Click for more information.

Rice Business Network
A Houston-based group whose members gather once a month to exchange business cards and ideas. For more information, contact Karl Maier.

Rice Alliance for Technology & Entrepreneurship
A collaborative effort among Rice's Schools of Engineering, Management, and Natural Sciences, the Alliance aims to create a forum for exchange and collaboration among Rice management, engineering, and science communities; and to be a vehicle for education and support of, and advice to, inventors, innovators and entrepreneurs in their pursuit of new business concepts.

Rice Engineering Alumni
Formed in 1938, the REA is the oldest alumni organization at Rice. The REA is dedicated to supporting the George R. Brown School of Engineering, and offers numerous social and educational opportunities on campus each year which provide networking opportunities for its members.

Rice University Business and Professional Women
A group of Rice alumnae and friends in Houston who provide opportunities for professional, social, and educational interaction. For information, contact Corrinn Davis '01.

LinkedIn
Network with fellow alumni on LinkedIn, a professional networking site that helps members access thousands of business contacts through first-, second- and third-degree connections.

Recruiting at Rice

If you're looking for bright, motivated employees — summer or permanent — let the Rice Career Services Center help you dip into the talented pool of Rice students! Comprehensive guidelines to recruiting at Rice are available through the Career Services website.

Career Counseling and Testing Services

Available through the Center for Student Professional Development (formerly Career Services).
