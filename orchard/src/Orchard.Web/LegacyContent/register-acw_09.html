Class Selection

Alumni College Weekend is $275 per person and a Saturday-only pass is $150 per person—or $95 for young alumni who graduated after 1999. The cost includes course fees, meals, and parking. Upon receipt of your registration we will send you confirmation and details to facilitate your arrival and check-in. The deadline to register online is March 9. Walk-up registration will be $300 for the weekend program, $165 for the Saturday-only pass and $105 for the young alumni Saturday-only pass. All classes are filled on a first-come, first-served basis.
