New York City

Join our Facebook Group and sign up for the New York Alumni Listserv!
These do not take the place of monthly emails that are sent to all local alumni and friends through the alumni office. They listserv provide an opportunity for more informal communication among alumni, who can post activities or opportunities that may be of interest to fellow alums.

Upcoming New York Events

Click here to see upcoming events in the New York area, or visit the events calendar to search for specific events. You can also see a list of regional leaders.

Please note: If you live in the New York area and are not receiving email updates and special event invitations from the alumni office, it means we do not have your current email address in our system. Please take a moment to update your email by visiting the online alumni directory. Some events are only advertised by email!
