Football Postseason Information

Congrats to the Rice Owls football team on winning the 2008 Texas Bowl!

Texas Bowl, Reliant Stadium
Tuesday, December 30, 2008
Rice vs. Western Michigan
7:00 p.m. Kickoff (Central)

Broadcast live on the NFL Network

Get the Latest News by Email!

Join the Bowl-Bound mailing list to keep track of Rice Owls football team in the postseason. Items posted will include game ticket information, pregame and other event information, and all the latest on the team. To sign up, please visit https://mailman.rice.edu/mailman/listinfo/bowl-bound.

Ticket Information

Tickets are available through the Rice Athletics website at www.RiceOwls.com, by calling the athletics ticket office at 713-522-OWLS, or in person at the ticket office at Rice Stadium, which is open from 8:30-5:00, Monday through Friday.

Commemorative Texas Bowl Rice Rally Towels are available with bowl tickets on the Rice Athletics ticketing website under "2008 Texas Bowl Alumni Fan Packages." The Rice Rally Towel is free with a $10 donation made to the Rice Annual Fund in conjunction with the purchase of Texas Bowl tickets.

Events for Rice Fans in Houston

If you will attend the bowl game, plan to join other Rice alumni, parents, students, friends, and fans for a pregame party at Reliant Center beginning at 4:30 p.m. Please visit our listing of Houston events for Rice fans to register online by December 26, and check back frequently for updates. We'll also host a breakfast at Brochstein Pavilion on the Rice campus the morning of the game.

Regional Game-Watching Parties

Can't make it to Houston? Regional volunteers across the country plan to host local game-watching parties for Rice fans. Please visit our listing of regional game-watching parties for details. If you are interested in hosting an event in your area, please contact the Rice alumni office at 800-742-3258. Game-watching parties will be posted as they are finalized.
