GIVE TO RICE

 

Welcome! This site is intended as a clearinghouse for volunteer opportunities at Rice, and seeks to reconnect and engage alumni, Rice parents, and friends of the university in meaningful volunteer opportunities related to Rice.

If you're an alumna or alumnus, Rice parent, or friend of the university who is interested in volunteering at Rice, you've come to the right place!

Rice's greatest asset is Rice people, and there are a number of ways for volunteers to get involved in the life of the Rice community by serving as a resource to the university and its various organizations, to current students, and to one another. Choose one of the categories listed to the right for a list of opportunities or click here to fill out our volunteer interest form.
   

If you're with a campus office or other Rice group and would like to include a listing on this site, contact LaCresha Lamb.
Special thanks to alumna volunteer extraordinaire Anne Baillio '62 for compiling much of the information in this site.
