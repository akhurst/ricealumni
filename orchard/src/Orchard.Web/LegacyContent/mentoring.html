Student/Alumni Mentoring Program

 
We are currently recruiting alumni and students from all classes to participate.
Each match is carefully considered to ensure the best possible interaction between students and alumni. Students may request to be paired based on interests other than career advancement.
We will attempt to pair graduate students with alumni bearing graduate degrees. If you are an alumna or alumnus and have a particular interest in mentoring a graduate student, please indicate this on the mentor information form.
After pairings are received, a reception will be held to make the initial meeting of alumni and students more comfortable. The reception is also a great place to discuss meeting up again.
An evaluation form for both parties will be sent out to measure the success of the pairing and refine the process for future mentoring endeavors by the ARA.
If at any time either party is unhappy with the pairing, please contact the alumni office and we will attempt a new match.
 
Benefits for alumni mentors are:
 
Involvement with the Rice University community at the most personal level
Influencing students with your real world experiences
Impacting the lives of students
 
Benefits for students are:
 
Insight into the working world from a former Rice student
Opportunities available to you in your career or major of choice
Alternative career paths relating to your interests and major
Making a great connection from "beyond the hedges"

We encourage everyone to enter into a relationship where you could meet or correspond with your mentor or student on a regular basis. Please let us know if there are any limitations on the time you are willing or able to commit to this program.

We will make every effort to pair a mentor with a student interested in a similar career, or with a similar major, if that is what the student prefers. If that is not possible, we will pair based on similar interests since the mentor will still be able to offer valuable insight into the working world.
