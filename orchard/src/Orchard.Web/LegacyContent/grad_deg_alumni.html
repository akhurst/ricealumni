Supporting Graduate Students
There are a variety of volunteer opportunities at Rice in support of students, including graduate students. You can get involved through selected departments, serve as a mentor, or offer career advice through the Rice Career Network (formerly the Alumni Contact Service). You can also donate gently-used household items for re-use through the Graduate Student Loan Closet. Visit our Supporting Students page for more information.

Graduate Alumni Fund
The GAF supports programs and services for graduate alumni and current graduate students, including alumni events, student-run programming, and improvements to graduate student facilities on campus.

Make a donation to the Graduate Alumni Fund
Please designate your gift as "other" and enter "Graduate Alumni Fund" under Special Instructions. You may also make a gift to the fund by contacting LaCresha Lamb in the alumni office at 713-348-4057.

Submit a proposal to receive funding from the Graduate Alumni Fund (right click to download)
Write a proposal for the program or service you would like the GAF to finance. Submit your proposal and contact information to the fund manager, Radu Filip '08, and you will be contacted shortly.
