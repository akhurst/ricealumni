Support REA

Every Rice alumnus who has a degree from one of the departments of the George R. Brown School of Engineering is member of the Rice Engineering Alumni.  All members are welcome at REA events.  Many events are free, while others have a nominal charge. 

REA members can enhance their support by becoming REA Sponsors.  As an REA Sponsor, your contribution will sustain and enhance REA programs and provide support to the School of Engineering.

Sponsorship Levels
The REA offers the following sponsorship levels: 

  • Blue Sponsor
    • Young Alumni (10 years or less since graduation):  $25
    • Alumni (more than 10 years since graduation):  $40
  • Gray Sponsor: $250
  • Patron:  $500
  • Benefactor:   $1000
  • Fellow:  $2500
  • Endowed Engineering Scholarship Donor:  $100,000

Effective July 2012, REA Sponsors receive the following benefits:

  • All sponsors receive the Rice Engineering annual magazine and free admission to those REA events which have admission charges. 
  • Sponsors at the Gray level and above receive additional recognition, commensurate with their level, at REA events. 
  • In addition, Fellows are invited to exclusive events with School of Engineering leaders.

Sponsorships at the Benefactor level and above directly fund the REA scholarship and grant programs.  The REA sponsorship year coincides with the Rice fiscal year, July 1 to June 30.

Become an REA Sponsor/ Renew your Support

Online:
Become an REA Sponsor using Rice University's secure giving site at http://giving.rice.edu/
Simply select "Rice Engineering Alumni (GF60)" from the designation menu when submitting your gift.  Providing this designation with your gift will automatically enroll you as an REA Sponsor at the corresponding level.  

By mail:

Send a check payable to “Rice University”, indicating “Rice Engineering Alumni (GF60)” in the memo field, to the following address:

Office of Alumni Affairs - MS 520
P O Box 1892
Houston, TX 77251-1892
