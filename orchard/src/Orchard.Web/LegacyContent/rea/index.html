Welcome!

Welcome to the home page of Rice Engineering Alumni (REA), the alumni organization for the George R. Brown School of Engineering at Rice University.

The goal of REA is to serve as a catalyst for beneficial collaboration and lifelong connections between alumni and the school.  REA pursues this goal by recognizing outstanding achievements, supporting students, and facilitating interactions among alumni, students, faculty and staff. 

The menu to the right will guide you through our website.

Every Rice alumnus who has a degree from one of the departments of the George R. Brown School of Engineering is a member of Rice Engineering Alumni.  REA members can enhance their support of the School by becoming REA Sponsors.  As an REA Sponsor, your contribution will sustain and enhance REA programs and provide support to the George R. Brown School of Engineering. 

I invite you to participate in REA events, get involved in REA programs, and support the mission of REA and the School of Engineering.

Very truly yours,
Kate Hallaway
CE '04
President, Rice Engineering Alumni
