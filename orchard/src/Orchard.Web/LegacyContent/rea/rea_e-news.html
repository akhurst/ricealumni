REA Launches E-Newsletter for Members

Beginning in March 2005, the REA will send out quarterly e-newsletters to current dues-paying and lifetime members. These periodic emails will provide an added incentive for membership in the REA and give members insights into the Brown School as well as provide advance notice of upcoming REA activities.

The e-newsletter is part of a larger effort by the REA to provide more electronic communication and interaction with our new website. If you have comments or suggestions, please contact Bruce Daniel '74.
