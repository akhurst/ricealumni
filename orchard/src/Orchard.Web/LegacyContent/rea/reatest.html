Welcome!

This web site contains information on the Rice Engineering Alumni (REA), the official alumni organization for the George R. Brown School of Engineering at Rice University. Here you can find out about upcoming REA activities, read news about engineering projects at Rice and throughout the U.S., and learn how to support the REA, the Brown School of Engineering, and current engineering students.

The menu to the right will guide you through our new website. If you are interested in getting more involved with the REA, please contact us by email.
