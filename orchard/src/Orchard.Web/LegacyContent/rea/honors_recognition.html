REA Honors:  Recognition Programs

Outstanding New Faculty

In 2010, REA began the practice of recognizing each year a faculty member in the School of Engineering who, although relatively new to Rice, has already brought considerable recognition and distinction to Rice and to the School.  The honoree is asked to give the keynote talk at the REA Fall Social. 

Outstanding New Faculty Honorees

2011:  John T. McDevitt, Brown-Wiess Professor of Bioengineering and Chemistry (joined Rice in 2009)
Click here for the event program from Dr. McDevitt’s presentation. 

2010:  Jamie E. Padgett, Assistant Professor of Civil & Environmental Engineering (joined Rice in 2007)

Board Resolutions

In 2009, REA began the practice of considering special resolutions in appreciation or in memory of certain faculty, staff, alumni and friends who have made exceptional contributions to Rice engineering.  These resolutions are intended to capture and express the sentiments of large segments of Rice engineering alums. 

To date, the REA Board of Directors has adopted resolutions in honor of the following individuals:

M. Kenneth Oshman ’62 (1940-2011)

BA 1962, BSEE 1963
Founder of ROLM Corporation
Benefactor of the Oshman Engineering Design Kitchen
Rice Board of Governors 1984-1988
Rice Board of Trustees 2004-2011
Distinguished Alumnus 1984
Outstanding Engineering Alumnus 1993
Adopted Sept. 15, 2011

Franz Brotzen (1915-2010)

Founding Professor of Materials Science 1954-2009
Dean of Engineering 1962-1966
Master of Jones and Brown Colleges
Association of Rice Alumni Gold Medal 1989
Adopted June 11, 2010

William “Dr. Bill” Wilson (1943-2009)

Professor of Electrical Engineering 1972-2006
Resident Associate of Wiess College 1978-2006
Adopted Jan. 28, 2009

Suggestions for REA Special Resolutions are welcome; please contact the Alumni Honors Chair
