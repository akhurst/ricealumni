Houston Young Alumni and Students

The Rice Houston Young Alumni Committee engages Houston-area young alumni (roughly 10 years from graduation) in fun and meaningful opportunities to maintain a lifelong relationship with the Rice community and Houston.

The Houston Young Alumni Committee (HYAC) plans events, programs, and services designed to introduce recent alumni to the Association of Rice Alumni and to meet their social and educational needs (e.g., cultural outings, pregame tents at sports events, networking mixers, Homecoming and Beer Bike activities, service opportunities, etc.). All young alumni are welcome to participate on the planning committee.

Builder's Award

The Houston Young Alumni organization also presents the Builder's Award, which recognizes recent alumni who have made significant contributions to the Rice community. Each year, a subcommittee of the Houston Young Alumni Committee will select the recipients of this award.
