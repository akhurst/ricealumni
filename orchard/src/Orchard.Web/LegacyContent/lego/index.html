Ages 8+

Are you ready to build your LEGO Lovett Hall? We appreciate your support of the Association of Rice Alumni and hope you enjoy your gift. Building instructions are available via the links below. Unlike at Rice, you will of course not be graded on this particular project, so please have fun!

The "LEGO Lovett Hall" was created using LEGO Factory. There are approximately 216 pieces. While it may seem like a lot of small pieces, we used the sizes and colors available to us. Just imagine how many bricks were used building the original Lovett Hall nearly 100 years ago!

Please note: It has come to our attention that the LEGO Factory made some minor substitutions when creating each Lovett Hall packet. You may find that instead of a single 1x4 white piece, you might instead have two 1x2 pieces, etc. If you have any questions while viewing the instructions, please contact the alumni office. Happy building!
