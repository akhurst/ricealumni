Around the World by Private Jet
An Exploration of the World's Greatest
Treasures & Legendary Places
 February 2-25, 2009

Dear Traveler,

Join us for an extraordinary 24-day journey around the world by private jet, to explore the most treasured and legendary places on Earth–places that have inspired and captured the imagination of humankind for centuries. more...

Itinerary

Lima, Cusco & Machu Picchu, Peru
Marvel at how Incan and Spanish cultures intertwine in Cusco, and visit Machu Picchu, the great citadel abandoned by the Inca and rediscovered in 1911.

Easter Island
 Delve into the mysteries of Easter Island's enormous moai statues, fascinating clues to the island's famously lost culture.
Apia, Samoa
Enjoy the hospitality and unspoiled beauty of tropical Samoa, considered to be the cradle of Polynesian culture.
Port Douglas & The Great Barrier Reef, Australia
Swim along the rainbow variety of marine life in this extensive coral reef system and marine sanctuary that is the world's largest World Heritage Area.
Angkor, Cambodia
Explore the astoundingly beautiful Angkow Wat temple complex, one of the world's great architectural triumphs, and discover the hidden treasures within.
Lhasa, Tibet
See the majestic Potala Palace in Lhasa–former home of the Dalai Lama, filled with countless rooms richly decorated with centuries of Buddhist artwork.
The Taj Mahal, Agra, India
Visit the legendary Taj Mahal at Agra, a lasting masterpiece of history's greatest builders, the Moguls.
Tanzania Safari Adventure
Enjoy a safari adventure in the Serengeti National Park–home to a staggering concentration of wildlife.
Luxor & Giza, Egypt
Admire Egypt's legendary antiquities, or visit the ancient lost city of the Nabataeans at Petra.
Fez, Morocco
Wander the colorful souks and explore the treasures of Saharan kings in the celebrated medina of Fez.

Cost Per Person
Double Occupancy: $56,950
Single Occupancy supplement: $8,545
