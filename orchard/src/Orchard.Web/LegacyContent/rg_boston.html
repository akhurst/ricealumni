Boston

Rice Alumni Boston re-launched in the spring of 2004 with a larger slate of leaders and some great new programs for alumni and friends, including quarterly happy hours, educational opportunities, social gatherings, and family-friendly events. The Boston Alumni Committee meets quarterly to share ideas and organize programs. If you would like to join the planning committee, please contact Nicole Peralta in the alumni office by email or at 713-348-4534.
